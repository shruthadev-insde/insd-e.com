INQUIRIES IN STRATEGIC DESIGN

[INSD]e* is a multidisciplinary design consultancy practice at its core. The practice brings together thinking from multiple disciplines to create thoughtful and contemporary work rooted in clarity, context and responsible decision making.

We specialise in design consultancy, visualisations and early stage design strategy for developers, builders and private clients. The practice works in collaboration with engineers, contractors, designers and researchers to deliver refined, contextual and logic driven design outcomes.

DESIGN  .  RESEARCH  .  CONSULTANCY  .  COLLABORATIONS
*
The name operates as a two-part system: INSD is the stable intellectual core; e* is a mutable suffix that adapts to context without fragmenting the identity. The asterisk signals openness. The practice begins with inquiry before it arrives at form.

INSD-eNV
environments
ARCHITECTURE & SPATIAL DESIGN CONSULTANCY
NATURE OF WORK

INSD-eNV is the primary built-environment arm of the practice. It covers the full scope of residential architecture, interior design, spatial strategy, developer consultancy, and site advisory from first brief to construction delivery.

The work begins before the drawing does. Every project starts with an understanding of the site, the client, and the context. Architecture here is not a product delivered at the end of a process. It is the process.
