The Accord Process
Before we recommend,
we listen.

The Accord asks you ten questions. Not about style references or mood boards — about how you live.

First: five questions about the shape of your domestic self. Then: a budget that determines what is possible. Then: five image questions that map your instincts across five axes — warmth, density, roots, formality, and the object.

At the end, Sky City Accord resolves a palette from nine possible interiors. Yours will have a name.
